Greek salad

Description:

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups uncooked orzo pasta
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans marinated artichoke hearts
  • 1 cucumber, seeded and chopped
  • 1 tomato, seeded and chopped
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 (2 ounce) can black olives, drained
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon lemon pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  2. Combine pasta, artichoke hearts, cucumber, tomato, onion, feta, olives, parsley, lemon juice, oregano, and lemon pepper in a large bowl; toss well. Chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  3. Just before serving, drizzle reserved artichoke marinade over salad.
